The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a bread of small scent hound, similar in appearence to the much arger foxhound. The beagle was feveloped primarily for hunting hare, known as beaging. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as detection dog of prohibited agrictultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. the beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
